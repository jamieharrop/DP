Rebecca Vaughan - Performer & Writer

Rebecca trained at ALRA following a degree in English Literature at Cardiff University. Film and television work includes the lead in Fearnort's feature film Grace's Land, the receptionist in The Interview and playing Jane Austen in The Dead, both for the BBC, and an array of short films.
Recent theatre credits include I, Elizabeth (Dyad Productions), The Picture of Dorian Gray (Bill Kenwright), Room 24 (Nuffield Theatre); Havisham (BAC); As associate artist with Lanternfish Theatre Company she played Hermia in A Midsummer Night's Dream and Mrs Hawkins in Treasure Island. She studied physical theatre with Sachi Kimura (student of Philippe Gaulier) and continues her wider training at both The Actors Centre and City Lit in Improvisation, Clowning (including Street Clowning), and Puppetry. She lives in St Albans with one husband and no cats.
